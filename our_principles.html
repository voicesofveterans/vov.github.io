Preserve. protect. Respect.

Our core principles are the backbone of our work

Preserve

Voices of Veterans is dedicated to preservation of the United States past in order to promote its future. Our mission is to record the stories of our nations veterans and protect these stories for the next generation of Americans. Voices of Veterans parterners with high schools to promote the interaction of teenagers and veterans. High schoolers involved in Voices of Veterans interview veterans inorder to better understand what our nation's greatest have been through to secure the future of the United States.

Protect

Voices of Veterans ensures the protection of our veterans stories'. Interviews are stored on Voices of Veterans servers, solid state storage devices, and Google servers. Through storage redundacies Voices of Veterans makes sure that these stories will be around for generations to come.

Respect

Voices of Veterans shares these veterans stories to give the American public an up close and personal view of how war effects our nations bravest soldiers. By shining a new light, we promote a new level of respect by allowing viewers to create a personal connection to our veterans.

Our Program

Voices of Veterans is a veteran and high school student engagement program. Through the interviewing processes, we expose students to a new level of public speaking and engagement that can help advance students succeed in future endeavors and in the classroom. We also encourage veterans to share their experiences and interact with a new generation. Our goal is to simply engage today and prepare for tomorrow.

Get in touch

Sign up to get updates when new stories are shared so you don't miss out on the voices of these amazing veterans! If you are a veteran and would like to share your story we would love to hear from you.
